You are Willy (the Wizard), a slightly crazy Wizard/Alchemist and a founding of member of Central League for Ordinance, Wizardry, and Necromancy (C.L.O.W.N).  wezcollect herbs, and resources in wild, or buy them at the market. As a part of the few good wizards left, you (and your cannon) have set out to besiege the various evil wizardly encampments, and restore C.L.O.W.N to it's former benvelolent self.

## The Idea (gameplay)

Collect magical goods from raided encampments, and the wild. Use them to brew different potions or other projectiles. Shoot them from your cannon at Angry Birds like structures trying to the crystals that power the dark wizards evil magic.
